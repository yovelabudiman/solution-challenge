Who are we?

Green Snap Solutions is dedicated to advancing the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 12 by championing responsible patterns of consumption and production. Our focus centers on motivating individuals to transition to renewable energy sources.

Through a straightforward incentive program, participants can earn vouchers by demonstrating a reduction in electricity and hot water consumption from the previous month. By facilitating the simple upload of utility bills, our initiative aspires to encourage a significant shift toward cleaner energy choices, fostering a more sustainable and environmentally friendly future. We invite everyone to be part of this pragmatic movement, making meaningful contributions to achieve a cleaner, better world.
